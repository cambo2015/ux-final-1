Restaurants > Koli

Koli

Koli is an island staple for any seefood adventurer. It means "delicious tongue". Koli is a seafood restaurant and is great for seafood connoisseurs. Wild Cod and Salamon are caught every day and fried up with rice and coconut juice. If you like good heart healthy food this is for you. Next time you go make sure to ask for "Cod with rice" as it is a staple here

Things to Know About Alcohol

  1. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m
  2. The drinking age on Taniti is 18.
  3. However the drinking age is not strictly enforced.
