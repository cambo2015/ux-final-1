Rangers

Rangers has all the things you would need. Almost everything is imported and that means the cost is higher. You can find it on the south side of the island. It is a great place to get your imported goods. Need Cereal and Milk? No problem. Need band-aids or mucus relief? This is the place. The staff is also extreemly friendly and are great to ask if you can't find what you are looking for. They will point you in the right direction.
