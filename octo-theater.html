Octo Theater

Come to the Octo Theater and see the latest movies! The massive IMAX screens are perfect for anybody who loves the big screen. In every seat you can push a button and you can raise and lower the leg rests. It is a fantastic place for 3D movies as well and boasts a Sony Atmos surround system as well. It you want to be entertained and see a movie this is it!
