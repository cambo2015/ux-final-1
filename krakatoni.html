Krakatoni Volcano Hike

If you want a long hike you can walk to the top of the Krakatoni Volcano. This volcano currently inactive and it is safe to walk there and is extreemly fun for all adventurers. You can see the entire island as it stands two thousand feet above sea level. It takes about 2.5 hours to reach the summit so be sure to bring plenty of water.
