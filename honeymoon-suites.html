Taniti Honeymoon Suites

123-664-5678

Taniti Honey Moon Suites have rooms on the open ocean. These suites are intimate and are wonderful you need a romantic getaway for two. The suites are clean and provide room service whenever you need it, and have all modern amenities for your personal needs. If you enjoy swimming in the ocean and are on a honeymoon this is the place!

Here in Taniti, we use the same 120 volts poweroutlets as the U.S.A, so be sure to bring a converter if you are not from the U.S.A.
