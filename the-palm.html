The Palm

This is a wonderful place to come and eat hamburgers which are delicious. A local favorite is "The Big One". It has a spicy cajun sauce and it leaves your mouth watering and wanting more. The burgers all come with juicy salted fries and dipping sauces. This is a must visit if you love American Food and hamburgers.
