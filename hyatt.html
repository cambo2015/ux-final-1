The Hyat Resort

123-555-3254

They Hyatt Resort is a wonderful hotel resort. If you are looking for a luxurious stay, this is your place. It has a pool by the open ocean and has an interior fit for kings. The beds are extreemly soft and utilize use memory foam to help you have a restful sleep. Enjoy the 50 inch screen TV in teach room and order room service whenever you need it. If you want to sleep in style, swim whenever you want, this is the place for you!

Here in Taniti, we use the same 120 volts poweroutlets as the U.S.A, so be sure to bring a converter if you are not from the U.S.A.
