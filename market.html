The Market

The Outdoor Market has local food you can buy. As a outdoor market, it has mangoes to leeks and everything in-between. If you are ever in the mood to buy produce this is the place because it is cheap. Produce is all less than a dollar because the food sold here is grown on the island.

Reviews

Mila Row

I live on the island and the produce here is so cheap. We can all sustain our familes for less than 10 dollars a meal!

Harry Hunter

I just got off the airplane and saw this place on the way to my hotel. We stopped and I bought coconuts for less than one dollar. Cheap place to get fruit. Much cheaper than the states.
